About Us

During these dark times with the worldwide pandemic COVID-19, our community has shifted our focus towards death rates and health precautions. Yet, with all this worry for our friends and family, we tend to overlook some of the people and establishments who suffer even more than we do; small businesses. Succeeding in the corporate world with small startup ideas or establishments is especially hard to do, as these companies lack the resources to advertise and market their ideas or products. That's why our mission at On The Rise is to provide a platform for these small businesses to sell their products, so they too can minimize the damage the coronavirus has done to them.
